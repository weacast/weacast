# XWind

The XWind application has been deployed at Airbus (opens new window) to support Flight Testing. This application aims at helping users to plan flight tests optimally by quickly identify airport runways matching specific business conditions (such as cross-wind (opens new window)) based on weather forecast data.

Watch the video (opens new window)

# Kano

The Kano application will be soon deployed at IRSN (opens new window) to support nuclear crisis management. This application aims at helping users to assess health risks optimally based on weather forecast data.
